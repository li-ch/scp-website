Role: Co-founder

About the Job:

We are building the leading fin-tech lending company in the Philippines and seeking exceptional leaders to join and spearhead the business.

You will have a unique opportunity to be responsible for establishing and leading the business, setting up the operations and building a world-class team. This individual should be entrepreneurial with a passion to build, and looking for a very challenging but rewarding experience. You should be able to work independently, and navigate an unstructured environment. We are looking for someone who can make things happen!

You will work as an entrepreneur, yet be supported financially and strategically by both local and international shareholding partners. The compensation will include competitive salary plus an equity stake in the business. You hold the key to unlock the full potential of your equity in the business.

Location: Manila, Philippines

About Us:

Stem Capital Partners and Esquire Financing Inc. are creating a joint-venture to establish Philippine’s leading fin-tech lending company. Stem Capital Partners is a Hong Kong based private investment firm focused on mid-cap companies in Asia Pacific. Esquire Financing Inc. is the leading financing company in the Philippines providing unsecured business loans to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Since inception in 2011, Esquire has released over PHP 12 billion (approximately USD $300 million) in loans and rediscounted checks with 3-yr past due loan average of under 3%.

Desired Skills and Experience:
  • 3-10 years of experience in top-tier investment bank / consulting firm or demonstrated success with a prior start-up
  • Track record of strong performance in academics and professional experience
  • Entrepreneurial and proactive mindset
  • High integrity and transparency
  • Resourceful and organized
  • Creative and commercial
  • Strategic and analytical
  • Ability to build, lead and manage teams
