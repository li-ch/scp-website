    INVESTMENTS

Esquire Financing Inc. is the leading financing company in the Philippines providing business loans to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Esquire provides unsecured business loans and rediscounting checks from PHP 100,000 to PHP 10,000,000 with average payment terms up to 1 year. The Company seeks to partner with its clients to help their businesses achieve sustainable growth. To date, Esquire has released over PHP 12bn (approximately USD 300M) in loans and rediscounted checks with 3-yr past due loan average of under 3%. Stem Capital made a strategic, minority investment and will sit on the Board to work closely with the management team.

Investment Date: 2014


GlassesGroupGlobal is Asia Pacific's leading online retailer for sunglasses, prescription glasses, and contact lenses with over 20,000 products and 150+ brands. The store offers a wide range of leading and new brands at the best prices. The Company currently has operations in Hong Kong, Malaysia, Taiwan, Philippines, Singapore, Australia, and New Zealand.

Investment Date: 2013


CompareAsiaGroup is an independent financial comparison site in Asia which offers free comparisons of personal loans, housing loans, credit cards, and insurance products. The Company partners with over 90 product providers offering over 1,500 products to compare with operations in Hong Kong, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Taiwan, and Thailand. The Company's expansion plan includes Singapore and Vietnam.

Investment Date: 2013

On April 22, 2015, CompareAsia Group secured $40M in Series A funding led by Goldman Sachs, Jardine Pacific. (Official press release)


Bullet Manila is a production house based in Manila focused primarily on consumer and luxury commercials for international and domestic clients. The rapid growth of Bullet Manila's business led to significant working capital requirements. Stem Capital worked with Bullet to structure and finance its receivables from international, blue chip clients.

Exited successfully in October 2014.
