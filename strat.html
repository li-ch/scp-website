FUNDAMENTAL COMMITMENT TO THE MID-CAP SECTOR

We exclusively and deliberately focus on mid-cap companies. There is a growing deal flow from emerging entrepreneurs, generational change and pre-IPO companies that are constrained by limited access to capital. We see significant investment opportunities to support these mid-sized companies.

DEDICATION TO GROWTH

Once we invest in a business, we are an active partner committed to our portfolio companies’ growth. We build strategic relations with management teams to provide operational and financial guidance. We support the execution of each portfolio company’s growth plan including scaling existing business, expanding product offerings or add-on acquisitions. We focus on governance and reporting to meet the highest international standards. We utilize our network of relationships to accelerate growth for our portfolio companies.

WE PROVIDE A PATHWAY FOR OUR PORTFOLIO COMPANIES TO ACCESS “LARGE CAPITAL”

We build leading middle-market businesses in partnership with strong management teams to maximize shareholder value. During our investment horizon, complete alignment with our management team is critical to success.

TARGET SIZE:
  • $25 – 75M equity investments in mid-cap companies
  • $2 - 10M in high growth special situation opportunities
TARGET PROFILE:
  • Strong fundamental business and cash flow
  • High integrity management team with demonstrated track record
  • Receptivity to strategic partnership
  • Realistic transaction expectations
INVESTMENT FOCUS:
  • Geography: South East Asia, Hong Kong, Pan Asia
  • Industry: Retail / consumer, financial services, business services, industrial, healthcare / education and food / agriculture
INVESTMENT STRUCTURE:
  • Control or strategic minority investments
  • Equity. Structural flexibility to mitigate downside via structured or convertible debt and other high-return securities
  • Discipline – only invest in high conviction situations
RETURNS:
  • Seek private equity returns (20%+)
  • Target investment period of 4-6 years
